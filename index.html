Frequently Asked Questions

What is the benefit of PPF in Coimbatore weather?

Coimbatore's heat and dust can degrade paint quickly. PPF blocks UV rays and prevents dust-induced swirls, keeping your car looking new for 10+ years.

Is PPF better than Ceramic Coating?

PPF offers physical protection against stone chips and scratches, while Ceramic Coating offers chemical resistance and gloss. For ultimate protection, we recommend both!
